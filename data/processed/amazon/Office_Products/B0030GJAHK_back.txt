The back of the chair can be moved to six heights. No, the back does not tilt independent of the seat. I found the back to offer more support than the typical mesh chair, especially in the lumbar area. I wanted the back to have some "give" so I could lean back a bit (and adjust the angle of the back for my body). The back of the chair does allow for adjustment, but the back is rigid and again not comfortable. The back can be adjusted up and down, but it's hard to adjust properly.
