Since you (obviously) can't tear off one of the upper 2 sections without taking the one beneath it, the elastic does come in handy since it can still hold those sections without just wasting them and throwing them away. Comprised of three perforated sections in each sheet, I'd recommend ignoring the To Do..., To Remember..., and To Buy... headings and writing your notes starting at the bottom of each page, so that you can tear off each third of the sheet as you've filled it or need to reference the information.
