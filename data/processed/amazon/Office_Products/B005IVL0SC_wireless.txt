The Epson WorkForce 645 Wireless is better for those who want wireless printing better than the workforce 630. my sons likes that you can put 250 sheets of paper in the tray, and that the 645 has a 30-page Auto Document Feeder to quickly copy, scan or fax piles of one or two-sided documents. If you do not have a wireless network or prefer to use a direct connection, you will need a usb printer cable before setting up your printer.
