I haven't compared the print speed or quality of the two yet, but when I do I will add to this review.So my initial impression is that the larger Artisan 837 is in some ways a superior machine. With the Epson I can not only print from all of my apps, I can also scan, copy, and access documents stored in the Cloud right from my iPad or iPhone. The print quality was excellent and the print ratio was fast. The print quality for photos was also excellent! The print quality is good enough for most needs (don't expect laser quality out of this guy), and the scans are decent quality. Before mounting the Install CD the basic print, copy and fax basic settings should be input through the printer control touch pad before attempting to set up a connection to the PC or network. Direct print on CD/DVDs is supported with the XP-800 but I did not try it.For photo reproduction, in my opinion the Expressions printer is the clear favorite. The print speed was acceptable but if I was printing out a 20 page document, I might get antsy in waiting.The ADF was able to scan multiple pages into a PDF without any issue.
