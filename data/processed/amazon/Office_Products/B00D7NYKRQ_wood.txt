The wood frame is not of the highest quality, so keep in mind the price you're paying reflects that. The wood is fake, as you should expect from an item in this price range. Close up, however, you can tell that the "wood" is basically a paper veneer. The &#34;wood finish frame&#34; is plastic. The wood frame is thin and the whole chalkboard is lightweight. The wood is a darker finish laminate. The wood does not have a quality finish in my opinion, the chalkboard portion is that 'chalk board paint' stuff. It would have been nice if it was actual wood, but it does look nice when it arrives. It has a decently wide wood frame around it, with a nice large open black chalkboard area: it's a good value, seems nice and sturdy, especially for the price.
