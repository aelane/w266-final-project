This Scotch PopUP Tape Handband Dispenser is fabulous for wrapping gifts easily and simply. But being able to wrap gifts without picking up and putting down the tape--and misplacing it--over and over again is the main function of this product, and it definitely does what it's supposed to do. They come in handy (pun intended) especially when wrapping Christmas gifts. I wouldn't wrap gifts without it. Wrapping several gifts under time pressure made me really appreciate this dispenser.
