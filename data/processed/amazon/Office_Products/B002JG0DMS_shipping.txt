However, I don't print the shipping address on them because UPS requires their own labels with barcodes and etc. But if you want a brown shipping label, this is a good choice. All while using these as shipping labels as well.These work great at covering up other labels. It allows the shipping information to stand out and that is what you need. If you like to reuse shipping boxes and quickly want to cover old labels, this does the job just fine.
