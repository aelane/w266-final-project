The printer easily joined my existing network and prints wirelessly without any glitches. )The printer the Brother replaced was a HP AIO that worked nicely for my needs - that is, basically wireless printing of text, and the occasional photocopying chore. this printer is HUGE.It also is a bit on the pricey side, another factor to consider.
