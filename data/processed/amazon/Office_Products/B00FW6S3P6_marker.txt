It does stay if the marker and clip and magnets are all off, but just barely. The marker is easy to use and the surface is smooth. The marker is easy to erase and writes cleanly. It shows the marker colors brightly, is easy to erase, and the markers write clearly on it. The included marker is black and attaches to the top via a clip. The marker includes and eraser. The marker does not write very well, but I had other dry erase markers available and they worked better, so it is not a problem with the board. Also the marker was completely dried out.
