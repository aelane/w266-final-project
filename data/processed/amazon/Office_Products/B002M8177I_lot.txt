But I don't know that I'll be using a lot of these, as it could get expensive fairly quickly. I wanted the Avery NoteTabs because I own a lot of cookbooks. I do a lot of paperwork. I reuse it and don't wast a lot of paper. If you can learn to reuse these over and over you can save a lot of paper and maybe help save a few trees. I do a lot of deposition and document review, and these let me make notes without marring the original. I tend to read a lot of non-fiction books.
