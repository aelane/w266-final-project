I wasn't really sure how these could be so much better than regular sticky notes that they merited their own name, but I do think these are a bit more sticky or &#34;super sticky.&#34; I have never had one fall off the wall on me or any other vertical surface.On the other end of the spectrum, I've also not had trouble removing one from delicate book pages.
