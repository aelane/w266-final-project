It's perfect for this calendar because the matchbox is about the same size as the calendar squares, allowing precise erasing.It's extremely useful being able to see four months of planning/appointments at a glance. Yes, I have a calendar on my phone but this is great to just quickly turn my head and see what my week looks like or even next month for that matter. This calendar is large enough to see but not so huge that it takes up your whole wall.
