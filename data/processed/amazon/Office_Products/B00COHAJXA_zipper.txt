This zipper binder is no different...it is larger & heavier than the traditional school binder.This is a very nice zippered binder with useful features, but Mead definitely needs more quality control on their production line  or maybe the design & fabric need  to be changed to make this binder more durable. The zipper is smooth and has several organization options inside it with a place for stationery as well. Inside, there's a zipper pocket for miscellaneous small items, and a two-inch three ring binder that is advertised to hold 180 sheets. It is a stylish zipper binder that is neither drab nor gaudy, and the various pockets and partitions are a great way to help keep things organized. The zipper doesn't feel too quality, and is poorly integrated into the design of the binder. At the top the zipper splays off and leaves a wide open gap that will not close, unlike the bottom part.
