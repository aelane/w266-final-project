I use dry erase markers in my work, and I must say I'm very pleased with these. We have several dry erase boards in the house for reminders and projects. Dry erase markers come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors - not to mention quality. My dry erase board has a narrow ledge on the bottom edge to put markers but unfortunately the ledge doesn't have much of a "lip" on it so my other dry erase markers were always rolling off and falling into my dog's water fountain. We use dry erase markers often in our house as my wife is a school teacher and she uses a whiteboard for teaching. No more Big Lots dry erase markers for me -- I'm staying with these. We have a dry erase board that our kids use and when these became available I picked up the set for them to use.
