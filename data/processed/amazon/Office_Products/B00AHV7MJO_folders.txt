I was interested in these because I have several hanging folders that don't have tabs at all. The folders are nice and strong and promise to be durable. Who said that hanging folders have to come with removable tabs, so you have to assemble three pieces to make a working folder? I have been using hanging folders for too many years to name at work as well as at home. First, the folders are well built hanging folders with metal hanging strips that run the full length of the top side-to-side. Some cheaper folders don't have full metal tabs, but these do.
