That said, it is a great little printer for general use. I got what I paid for - an inexpensive printer that does some things very well and other things not so well. This printer had rave reviews and we bought this for our son. He lives elsewhere but loves the printer. If your MAC is using OS 10.4 or earlier, this printer will not work for you.
