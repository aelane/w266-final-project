The safe is fairly heavy (20.4 pounds) and they put what looks and feels like a very lightweight, cheezy handle on it, that is not comfortable to hold. I'm sure that this safe isn't meant to be carried around like a briefcase, but I'd still prefer a more rugged and comfortable handle.All that having been said, I think it's a good safe for the price if you have a small amount of papers and whatnot that you want to make sure is safe from fire and/or flood. Keep the safe closed and locked to get the benefits of the fire and water protection and keep your two keys separated and not inside the safe. This safe has a nice handle on it for any burglar to carry it off. Fire will seal this safe and it will need to be pried open but the safe has an &#8216;after fire guarantee&#8217; and will be replaced by Sentry if it is in a fire.The safe is not for guns, medication or combustible materials. The safe is meant to sit flat rather than on end for maximum protection.
