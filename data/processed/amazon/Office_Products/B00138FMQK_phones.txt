The phones are comfortable to hold, look good, and are really easy to use and understand. This can be done from any of the phones or from the base unit. Although the KX-TG9344 is not perfect, it is the best of the phones I've looked at so far.It utilizes DECT 6.0, which is the latest technology for cordless phones and is supposed to have greater clarity and privacy without having interference issues like the older 2.4 GHz phones. The Panasonic phones may cost a little more than other brands, but in our experience they are well worth it. I purchased these phones after returning a set of Uniden cordless that I put up with for 2 years. I would definitely recommend these phones to anyone who needs a quality multi-handset system for the home. I have had these phones for about a year now and they have not failed us. Set up for the phones was also easy to follow and very logical. The phones will also freeze up and go blank from time to time, a few moments on the charger will normally fix it but it is a hassle.
